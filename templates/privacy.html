Overview

Free Tee Time Alerts ("we", "us", "our") operates the website www.freeteetimealerts.com. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

When you create a tee time alert, we collect the following information:

Phone Number: Used solely to send you SMS notifications when a tee time matching your alert criteria becomes available.

Alert Preferences: The golf course, date, and time window you select for your alert.

How We Use Your Information

Your phone number is used exclusively to send tee time availability alerts that you have requested. We do not use your phone number for marketing, advertising, or any other purpose.

SMS Messaging

By providing your phone number and creating an alert, you consent to receive SMS messages from us regarding tee time availability. Message frequency depends on the alerts you create. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your phone number is used only to deliver the alerts you request through our SMS provider (Twilio).

Data Retention

Your alert data is stored only as long as the alert is active. Once an alert is triggered or deleted by you, the associated data is removed.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@freeteetimealerts.com.
